  • 75% - Human
  • 10% - Elven
  • 10% - Dwarven
  • 5% - Other

Much of the regions economy is focused on collection of goods from the nearby Lake Sabastian and from the hills and forests which surround it.

The region focuses mostly on agriculture and have become mostly self-sufficient, priding themselves on requiring little imports from the rest of Maldantis.

Economic Breakdown
  • Agriculture
  • Aquaculture
  • Fishing
  • Lumber
  • Mining

Most people of Aristeium and Lorn speak primarily common, though many merchants will learn a small amount of elven to trade with the odd wood elf who makes their way south from Nalia.

The state religion of Maldantis is the Church of Taro, though Lorn is known for its independence so there is a sizable population of Orthodox Aeinists.

Due to the regions reliance and almost veneration of the lake, many members of the Church of Ul feel welcome.

In contrast to much of the surrounding region Aristeium and Lorn are ruled by a dual-government.

The ancient monarchy of Aristeium has maintained their rule while allowing for the church to act as a second chair. As such, the region is ruled by Sevtus royal family and the current High Pontiff Malik.

  • Molas: Northern port town tied closely to Aristeium, serves as their connection to the lake.
  • Senca: An outlying town which connects Aristeium to the northern and more rural regions of Lorn, also functions as a port town.

As would be expected much of the cuisine of the region is focused on fish and other products of the lake.

There have been many influences from Nalia, though the people take pride in their culture and often see elven dishes as not robust enough.

Biome: Temperate Broadleaf Forest

The city and its surrounding region boasts a very diverse array of different landscapes, with ore-filled hills to the north-east, forests blocking them from a great lake to the north and fertile planes to the south.

The region has expansive iron, coal, and quartz mines in the north-east.

Lake Sabastian acts as an end for Erala Sent and is a repository for many of the minerals and nutrients which promote a large and healthy fish population.

The cold waters do lead to the region having colder winters than other regions to the west and east.

The region is often cool and temperate with regions away from Lake Sabastian becoming progressively dryer and dryer with the region immediately around the lake hosting very wet winters and autumns.

The region around Aristeium holds a very diverse set of biomes which leads to the region being to host various types of flora.

Local Plants
  • Lake Sabastian Bowler Lily: This lily is known to cover large sections of the coast of Lake Sabastian, blooming all at once during early summer. After they bloom the flowers and their pads die and return the following summer.

Similar to the rest of Maldantis, many of the regions native magical and unique beasts have been hunted to near extinction with most of the animals left are more common native or invasive subtypes.

Local Animals
  • Lake Sabastian Rock Trout: This trout is farmed and fished extensively in the region. It is seen as a local delicacy though many outsiders find the taste of the fish to be dry and grainy.
