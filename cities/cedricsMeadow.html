Being an outlier in mainland Hogreve in a largely open centralized plains-land with a large river running nearby makes this an agricultural hotspot. Much of the income of the town and the surrounding villages is taken up entirely by farming though there is a sizeable market for retail goods, mostly just to fuel the agricultural heart.

Most of the goods produced here are sent to Barpool and other coastal settlements that are not able to sustain themselves on their own farms.

Economic Breakdown
  • Agriculture
  • Trade
  • Lumber
  • River Work

All of those that live in Cedric's Meadow know and speak common regularly, though many take up Elven as a second language to assist in trade and to converse with the sizeable population of elves which live in the region.

Surprisingly, Dwarvish is also popular and many will take words from the language and use them regularly without taking on the language for common speech.

Due to their proximity to Maldantis the town and region has a significant number of members of the Church of Taro, though due to the fact that they are not in the demographical region or in a kingdom which cites the religion as their national religion, most people are Orthodox Aeinists. Aeinists outnumber Tarans 2:1.

Cedric's Meadow sits on the outskirts of the large economic and governmental region of Barpool meaning that it falls under the governance of the East Barpool Company. It is a very important town both as a bread-basket and as a defensive position since it sits in the valley between Barpool and Maldantis.

  • Fort Vinning: A large fork and surrounding Hamlet which was, at one point, a major choke point in the various wars between Barpool and Maldantis. Still a heavily guarded and fortified location.

Being a ways away from the coast, Cedric's Meadow does not share the culinary pallet of the rest of the nation, instead it relied much more on staple crops and more simple stews and dishes constructed from what is readily available.

Biome: Temperate Broadleaf Forest

Much of the town is surrounded by large expanses of forest, with the region adjacent to Erala Tell being mostly cleared for lumber and for road construction. The towns exist in a lowland between two larger regions to the north and south which hold the northern tip of the Itruskin Forest and the bulk of the Ir'anya Forest to the north.

The region does not specialize in mining but there are some small iron and coal mines scattered along Erala Tell.

Cedric's Meadow was built directly adjacent to Erala Tell, a major waterway which leads to Barpool. The river is used extensively as a means of transporting goods.

Climate: Cool Temperate Moist

Lying in a low valley near a river, the rains from the coast are often funneled into the region, making it very wet, which promotes farming.

Due to the moistness of the region, the forests host large trees and ample moss.

local plants
  • Willow Moss: Long drooping moss which hangs from trees, giving them a distinct willow tree look.

The region is a hotspot for interesting creatures. The forests and hills create ample space for larger monsters and beasts to hide and hunt and with limited hunting in the regions further into the forests, they have been able to flourish for thousands of years.

local animals
  • Guildman's Owlbear: One of the few locations in the world where the Owlbear population is stable and they are not threatened. This is because this family of Owlbear do not have the solitary instincts of their cousins and instead form parliaments of Owlbears averaging around 6 adults with 3-4 young.
